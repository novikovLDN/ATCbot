# Incident Legal Mode

This document defines legal procedures for security incidents and data breaches.

## Breach Classification

### Data Breach

**Definition:**
- Unauthorized access to user data
- Unauthorized disclosure of user data
- Data loss or corruption
- Security incident with data impact

**Examples:**
- Database breach
- Credential compromise
- Unauthorized access to user data
- Data exfiltration

---

### Security Incident

**Definition:**
- Security event with potential impact
- Unauthorized access attempt
- System compromise
- Security vulnerability exploitation

**Examples:**
- Failed authentication attempts
- Unauthorized access attempts
- System compromise
- Security vulnerability

---

## Notification Thresholds

### GDPR Notification

**When to Notify:**
- Personal data breach
- High risk to data subjects
- Within 72 hours of discovery

**Who to Notify:**
- Supervisory authority (within 72 hours)
- Data subjects (if high risk)

**Notification Content:**
- Nature of breach
- Categories of data affected
- Number of data subjects
- Likely consequences
- Measures taken

---

### Customer Notification

**When to Notify:**
- Data breach affecting customers
- Security incident with customer impact
- Within 24-48 hours of discovery

**Who to Notify:**
- Affected customers
- Enterprise customers (immediate)
- Status page (public)

**Notification Content:**
- Incident description
- Impact assessment
- Remediation steps
- Contact information

---

## Legal Escalation Path

### Level 1: Internal Legal

**Triggers:**
- Data breach suspected
- Security incident with data impact
- Legal questions arise

**Actions:**
- Internal legal review
- Initial assessment
- Documentation collection

---

### Level 2: External Legal

**Triggers:**
- Confirmed data breach
- Regulatory notification required
- Legal action possible

**Actions:**
- External legal counsel engaged
- Regulatory notification prepared
- Legal strategy developed

---

### Level 3: Regulatory

**Triggers:**
- Regulatory notification required
- Regulatory investigation
- Legal action initiated

**Actions:**
- Regulatory notification submitted
- Regulatory cooperation
- Legal defense prepared

---

## Communication Freeze Rules

### When to Freeze Communication

**Triggers:**
- Legal action initiated
- Regulatory investigation
- Criminal investigation
- Legal counsel advice

**Rules:**
- No public statements
- No customer communication (without legal approval)
- No internal communication (without legal approval)
- All communication through legal counsel

---

### Communication Approval

**Required Approvals:**
- Legal counsel approval
- Executive approval
- Regulatory approval (if applicable)

**Process:**
1. Draft communication
2. Legal review
3. Executive approval
4. Regulatory approval (if needed)
5. Communication sent

---

## Incident Response Legal Mode

### Activation

**Triggers:**
- Data breach confirmed
- Legal action initiated
- Regulatory investigation
- Legal counsel advice

**Process:**
1. Activate legal mode
2. Engage legal counsel
3. Freeze communication
4. Document everything
5. Preserve evidence

---

### Legal Mode Procedures

**Documentation:**
- All actions documented
- All communications logged
- All evidence preserved
- Timeline maintained

**Communication:**
- All communication through legal counsel
- No public statements
- No customer communication (without approval)
- No internal communication (without approval)

**Investigation:**
- Legal counsel leads investigation
- Technical team provides support
- Evidence preserved
- Timeline maintained

---

## Evidence Preservation

### What to Preserve

**Technical Evidence:**
- Logs (all logs)
- Database snapshots
- System state
- Network traffic (if available)

**Documentation:**
- Incident timeline
- Communication logs
- Decision records
- Action items

**Legal Evidence:**
- Legal communications
- Regulatory notifications
- Customer notifications
- Legal opinions

---

### Preservation Process

**Steps:**
1. Identify evidence
2. Preserve evidence (no modification)
3. Document preservation
4. Secure evidence
5. Maintain chain of custody

---

## Post-Incident Legal

### Legal Review

**Required:**
- Legal review of incident
- Legal review of response
- Legal review of communication
- Legal review of documentation

**Deliverables:**
- Legal opinion
- Regulatory compliance assessment
- Legal risk assessment
- Recommendations

---

### Regulatory Compliance

**Requirements:**
- Regulatory notifications submitted
- Regulatory cooperation
- Regulatory compliance verified
- Regulatory follow-up

---

## Notes

- ⚠️ **Legal mode is serious** - Activate only when necessary
- ⚠️ **Communication freeze is mandatory** - No exceptions
- ⚠️ **Evidence preservation is critical** - No modification allowed
- ⚠️ **Legal counsel is required** - No legal decisions without counsel
